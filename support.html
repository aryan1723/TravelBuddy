Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I use the Voice-to-Voice translation?
Navigate to the dashboard or feature list, select "Voice to Voice Translation". Choose your source and target languages from the dropdown menus. Tap the "Start Speaking" or microphone button, speak clearly, and the app will provide the audio translation in the selected target language. Ensure your microphone permissions are enabled for the app.
How can I check my PNR status?
Go to the dedicated "PNR Status" page or feature (accessible from the dashboard or menu). Enter your 10-digit PNR number accurately in the provided field and click the "Check Status" or "Submit" button. The latest booking status, including seat confirmation, coach details, and charting status (if available), will be displayed.
Is my personal data safe with TravelBuddy?
Yes, we prioritize your data security. We collect minimal necessary data for app functionality and use encryption and standard security protocols to protect it. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. For complete details, please review our full Privacy Policy accessible below.
How accurate is the live train status?
The live train status is updated frequently based on real-time GPS tracking data and information sourced from railway authorities. While we aim for high accuracy, minor delays or discrepancies might occur due to network issues or sudden operational changes. It's a reliable guide, but always cross-verify with station announcements for critical, last-minute information.
What languages are supported for translation?
TravelBuddy supports translation between multiple major Indian and international languages. The exact list may be updated periodically. Please check the language selection dropdowns within the translation features (Voice-to-Voice, Voice-to-Text, Image-to-Text) for the currently supported languages.
